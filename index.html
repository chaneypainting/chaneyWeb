About

Welcome to Chaney Painting, we are the Sierra Mountains leading painting service company located in Mariposa. After completing a number of residential, commercial, interior, and exterior painting jobs, what we've learned is that what matters most is value - doing a quality job at an affordable and fair price. Approaching every project with the same uncompromising level of care, attention to detail, and professionalism, that concept of value is what we strive to deliver and we can tell it's working by the smiles on our customers' faces.
